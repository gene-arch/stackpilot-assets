Key Training Modules

Explore my core training areas. Click on each module to see how I empower your managers at every stage of the employee lifecycle.

Recruitment Lifecycle

I will guide managers through the entire recruitment process within ELMO, from creating and posting job requisitions to reviewing applications, communicating with candidates, and managing the hiring pipeline effectively. My goal is to streamline hiring and ensure a consistent candidate experience.

Efficient Onboarding

This module covers how to efficiently onboard new hires using the platform. I focus on initiating the onboarding workflow, assigning tasks to new employees and other team members, and ensuring all necessary compliance documentation is completed and stored digitally for easy access and reporting.

Daily HR Core Functions

I will train managers on the essential day-to-day HR functions available in ELMO. This includes accessing and updating employee data, utilising the employee directory, and understanding how to access key HR documents and information.

Learning Management

I will train managers on how to use ELMO to assign and track training courses, monitor employee learning progress, and access reporting to ensure their teams are up-to-date with their professional development.

Performance Management

This module focuses on how to leverage ELMO for effective performance management. I will cover setting and tracking goals, conducting performance reviews, providing ongoing feedback, and utilising analytics to manage team performance and development.
